 Asynchronous Methods for Model-based Reinforcement Learning
Yunzhi Zhang*, Ignasi Clavera*, Boren Tsai, Pieter Abbeel
[arXiv] [Webpage] [Code]
CoRL, 2019 (Spotlight). NeurIPS Deep RL Workshop, 2019.

Autonomous skill acquisition of robotics requires time and data-efficient methods due to the non-negligible cost of data collection. We proposed an asynchronous framework which improves time and sample efficiency, and further encourages exploration and reduces policy overfitting.
